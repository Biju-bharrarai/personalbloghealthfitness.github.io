About us

   1.What is Fitness?

Fitness is any kind of activity that keeps the body in a certain state and stimulates various body systems. On the other hand, health involves everybody system and can only be achieved through a healthy lifestyle.For instance, if a client of mine tells me that they haven't eaten well, ignored the amount of fat in their food, and mostly eaten processed foods, all the exercise in the world won't help.

   2.Why Fitness?

Chemical damage, weakening of the immune system, and depleted food weakness can't be fixed by exercise. Good health can only be supported by good nutrition. Fitness, of course, can help with health and WILL help improve health if it is a way of life. The majority of our health is the result of thousands of daily dietary choices. Ask yourself this straightforward question as you progress on your personal health journey or just get back on track:Am I just fit, or am I on the path to health and fitness?If your response is "just fit," you might want to try incorporating other aspects of wellness into a more comprehensive plan that focuses on spiritual, mental, emotional, and physical health together.

   3.Benefit of Health and Fitness.

One of the most important things you can do for your health is engage in regular physical activity.Being physically active can help you manage your weight, improve your brain health, lower your risk of disease, strengthen your bones and muscles, and make it easier to do things you normally do. There are some health benefits for adults who sit less and engage in any amount of moderate-to-vigorous physical activity. Physical activity is one of only a few lifestyle choices that has such a significant impact on health.
